# People

## **Overview**
The **People** section in 5day.io is where you manage all users within your organization’s account.

From the People section, you can:

- **Invite a New User** – Add new team members to your 5day.io account.
- **View and Manage Profiles** – Access user profile information.
- **Bulk Import Users** – Quickly upload multiple users at once through a bulk import feature.
- **Remove Users** – Remove people who are no longer part of the organization.
- **Search for Users** – Search users by **Name**, **Email Address**, or **Account Role** to quickly locate specific users.
- **Filter Users** – Apply filters to view users based on:
  - **Status**: Invited, Active, Inactive, Created.
  - **Account Role**: Account Owner, Account Admin, Manager, Member, External Member, Guest.
- **Sort Users** – Sort the list of users based on:
  - **Name**
  - **Email Address**

All people management activities—adding, viewing, updating, and removing users—can be performed easily from this section.

## **How to Access the People Section**
To access and manage the People section in 5day.io:

1. **Go to Account Settings:**\
   Click on your **Profile Icon** and select **Account Settings**.
1. **Select People:**\
   Inside Account Settings, click on the **People** option from the sidebar.
1. **Manage Users:**\
   After entering the People section, you will be able to:
   1. Invite new members
   1. View, edit, or deactivate user profiles
   1. Perform bulk imports
   1. Remove users if needed

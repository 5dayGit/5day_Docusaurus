# **How to Change the Account Owner**
To change the Account Owner in 5day.io, users must go to their **Account Profile**.\
 ([*Learn how to access your Account Profile*]()

Inside the Account Profile:

1. Click on the **Profile** tab.
1. Under the **Account Owner** section, you will see the current account owner's name along with the option to **Change Owner**.
1. Click on the **Change Owner** button.

You will now have two options:

- **Select from Existing Members:**\
  A list of all active members will appear. You can select an existing member from this list to make them the new account owner.
- **Invite a Member via Email:**\
  If the person you want to assign as the new owner is not already part of your account, click on **Invite a Member via Email**.
  - Enter the email address of the new member.
  - Once they accept the invitation and join, you can select them as the new account owner.

After selecting or inviting the new owner, confirm the change to complete the process.

## How to Access Account Profile

To access your Account Profile:

1. **Click on your Profile Icon** located at the bottom-left corner of the navigation panel.
1. From the menu that appears, select **Account Settings**.
1. Inside Account Settings, click on the **General** tab.
      ### IMAGE
1. Under **General**, you will find the option for **Account Profile**.
1. Click on **Account Profile** to view and manage:
   1. **Profile Settings** (Account Name, Country, Description, Logo, etc.)
   1. **Display Settings** (Time Zone, Language, Layout, Theme, etc.)

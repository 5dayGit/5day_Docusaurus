## **Remove a User**
1. **Go to Account Settings:**\
   Click on your **Profile Icon** at the bottom-left (or top navigation bar) and select **Account Settings**.
1. **Open People Section:**\
      ### IMAGE
   In Account Settings, click on the **People** tab to view all users added to your account.
1. **Locate the User to Remove:**\
   Browse the list of users and find the specific user you want to remove.
1. **Click the Three-Dot Menu (⋮):**\
   Next to the user’s name, click on the **three-dot menu** icon.
1. **Select Remove:**\
      ### IMAGE
   From the dropdown options, click on **Remove**.
1. **Confirm Removal:**\
   A confirmation prompt will appear. Confirm the action to permanently remove the user from your 5day.io account.

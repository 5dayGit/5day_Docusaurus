## **Change the Organization Logo**
To change the organization logo in 5day.io, users need to go to their **Account Profile**.

Inside the Account Profile:

1. Click on the **Display** tab.
1. In the Display tab, you will see the current **Organization Logo**.
1. Hover over the logo — a **pencil (edit) icon** will appear.
1. Click on the **pencil icon** to open logo options.
1. You will see two options:
   1. **Change Image** – Upload a new logo from your computer.
   1. **Remove Image** – Remove the current logo if you want to clear it.

After uploading the new image, simply submit to save the changes.

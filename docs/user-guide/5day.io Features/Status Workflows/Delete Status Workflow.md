## Overview
Deleting a **Status Workflow** removes it from the workspace.

A Status Workflow cannot be deleted if it is mapped to a **Task Type**.
## **How to Delete Status Workflow**
1. Log in to [**5day.io**](http://5day.io/).
1. Go to the workspace where you want to delete the Status Workflow.
1. Click the **three-dot (⋮) icon** next to the workspace name.
1. Click **Workspace Settings**.
1. Click **Customization**.
1. Click **Status Workflow**.
1. Locate the Status Workflow you want to delete.
1. Click the **three-dot (⋮) icon** next to the workflow name.
1. Click **Delete**.
1. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation popup.

**Note:** If the workflow is mapped to a **Task Type**, it cannot be deleted.

The system shows the message: *Could not delete workflow as it is mapped to a Task Type.*

To delete it, go to **Workspace Settings → Customization → Task Type**, remove the workflow mapping, and then delete the workflow.

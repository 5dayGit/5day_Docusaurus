## Overview

Editing a **Status Workflow** allows you to update the workflow name and manage the statuses used to track tasks within a workspace.
## **How to Access**
1. Log in to [**5day.io**](http://5day.io/).
1. Go to the workspace where you want to edit the Status Workflow.
1. Click the **three-dot (⋮) icon** next to the workspace name.
1. Click **Workspace Settings**.
1. Click **Customization**.
1. Click **Status Workflow**.
1. Select the workflow you want to edit.
## **Edit Status Workflow Name**
1. Click on the **workflow name** at the top.
1. Update the name.
1. Click **Save** to apply the changes.
1. Click **Discard** if you do not want to save the changes.
## **Edit a Status**
### **Rename a Status**
1. Under the relevant stage, click on the **status name**.
1. Update the name.
1. Click **Save**.
### **Change Status Color**
1. Click on the **color dot** next to the status.
1. Select a new color.
1. Click **Save**.

Note: Renaming a status or changing its color updates it everywhere the status is used across the workspace.
## **Add a New Status**
1. Under the required stage, click **+ Add Status**.
1. Search for an existing status,

or type a new name and click **+ Create New Status**.

1. Click **Save**.
## **Delete a Status**
1. Click the **three-dot (⋮) icon** next to the status.
1. Click **Delete**.

If the status is already used in tasks, the system shows a **Remap Status** screen.

1. Review the **Old Status**.
1. Select a **New Status** to replace it.
1. Click **Save**.

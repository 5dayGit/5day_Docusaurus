# Delete Client
In 5day.io, you can delete a client if it is no longer needed in your system.\
\
**Steps to Delete a Client:**

1. Go to **Account Settings**.
1. Click on **Clients** from the sidebar.
1. You will see a list of all the clients created.
1. Find the client you want to delete.
1. Click on the **three-dot menu** (â‹®) next to the client's name.
1. Select **Delete** from the dropdown options.
1. A confirmation box will appear asking for final confirmation.
1. Click **Confirm** to proceed with deleting the client.

**Important Notes:**

- **If a client is associated with any project, it cannot be deleted.**\
  You must first remove or reassign the associated projects before deleting the client.
- Deleting a client is **permanent** and cannot be undone.

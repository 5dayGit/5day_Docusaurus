# Create Client
In 5day.io, you can easily create and manage clients at the account level to organize your projects better.
### **Steps to Create a Client:**
1. Go to **Account Settings**.
1. Click on **Clients** from the sidebar.
1. Click the **+ Add** button at the top.
1. In the form that appears:
   1. Enter the **Client Name** (mandatory).
   1. Upload a **Client Logo** (optional).
1. Once you enter the details, the client will be automatically created and listed under the Clients section.

# Discussion View

## Subscription 

The ‘Discussion’ feature is **available across all subscription plans** in 5day.io.

## Availability of Discussion in Structure

Discussions are available at four levels of structure in 5day.io:

1.  Project
2.  Section
3.  Task
4.  Subtask

At the Project and Section, the **Discussion View** needs to be enabled in the **Project Settings** under the **Views** section.  

At the **Task** and **Subtask** levels, the **Discussion** feature is automatically enabled.

## Enabling Discussion View

By default, in all projects & section; discussion view is enable. Follow these simple steps to manage the visibility of discussion view in project/section views panel.

1.  Navigate to the **Project Settings**.
2.  Under the **Views** section, ensure that the **Discussion View** is enabled.
3.  Once enabled, discussions will be available at the **Project** and **Section**.
4.  For **Task** and **Subtask**, the discussion feature is auto-enabled and will be accessible without additional setup.
5. You can also change the order of the view to display in project views panel.

## Who Can Access

**Discussion View Permission:** Users with **view permissions** for discussions can access them at all levels (Project, Section, Task, Subtask).

## How to Access

**For Project/Section:**

1.  Go to the specific **Project** or **Section**.
2.  Select the view **Discussion** from project's top bar.
3.  You will be able to view all comments.

**For Task/Subtask:**

1.  Go to the specific **Project/Section**.
2.  Select the **Task** or **Subtask**.
3.  The task details page will open.
4.  Click on **Discussion** under the Task/Subtask details.
5.  You will be able to view all comments.

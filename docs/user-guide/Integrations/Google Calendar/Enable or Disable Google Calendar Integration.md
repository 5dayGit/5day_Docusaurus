## How to Enable or Disable Google Calendar Integration at Account Settings Level

Google Calendar integration must first be enabled at the Account Settings level before users can connect their individual Google accounts.

To enable the integration:

1. Log in to [5day.io](http://5day.io/).
1. Click on your **Profile icon**.
1. Go to **Account Settings**.
1. From the left panel, click **Integration Center**.
1. Locate **Google Calendar**.
1. Turn the toggle **ON** to enable the integration.

Once enabled, users (based on their role and permissions) will be able to connect their Google Calendar from **My Settings → Integrations**.

To disable the integration:

1. Go to **Account Settings → Integration Center**.
1. Turn the Google Calendar toggle **OFF**.

When disabled:

- Users will not be able to connect their Google Calendar.
- Existing connections will stop syncing.
- Google Calendar events will no longer appear in My Calendar.

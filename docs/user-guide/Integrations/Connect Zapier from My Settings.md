## How to Connect Zapier from My Settings (User-Level)

After Zapier is enabled at the account level, each user must connect their own Zapier account.

This connection authorizes Zapier to access your [5day.io](http://5day.io/) account for automation workflows.
### **To connect Zapier**
1. Log in to [5day.io](http://5day.io/).
1. Click your **Profile icon**.
1. Select **My Settings**.
1. From the left panel, click **Integration Center**.
1. Locate the **Zapier** card.
1. Click **Connect**.

You will be redirected to the Zapier authorization screen. Sign in to your Zapier account (if required) and grant the requested permissions to complete the connection.

Once connected, the Zapier card will show the status as **Connected**.

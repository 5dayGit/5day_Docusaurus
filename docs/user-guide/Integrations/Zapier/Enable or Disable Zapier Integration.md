## How to Enable or Disable Zapier Integration (Account-Level)

Zapier must be enabled at the account level before users can connect their individual Zapier accounts.

If the integration is disabled, users will not be able to use Zapier with [5day.io](http://5day.io/).
### **To enable Zapier integration**
1. Log in to [5day.io](http://5day.io/).
1. Click your **Profile icon**.
1. Select **Account Settings**.
1. From the left panel, click **Integration Center**.
1. Locate the **Zapier** card.
1. Turn the toggle **ON** to enable the integration.

Once enabled, users can connect their Zapier account from **My Settings → Integration Center**.

### **To disable Zapier integration**
1. Go to **Account Settings → Integration Center**.
1. Locate the **Zapier** card.
1. Turn the toggle **OFF**.

When Zapier is disabled:

- Users will not be able to use Zapier workflows with [5day.io](http://5day.io/).

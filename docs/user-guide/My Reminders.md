## **Reminders in 5day.io**
## Overview
**Reminders** in 5day.io help you stay on top of your schedule‚Äîwhether it's for personal tasks, project deliverables, or team follow-ups. You can create reminders for any important activity and get notified at the right time to take action.

Reminders can be created independently or linked directly to a specific **task or subtask**, offering flexibility to organize your day or plan work-specific updates.
### **Reminder Types**
- **Event Reminders** (e.g., personal or meeting alerts):\
  When creating a new standalone reminder, users are shown a simple **Date** and **Time** picker to set when the reminder should trigger.
- **Task/Subtask Reminders**:\
  When linked to a task or subtask, the **Date** field includes more contextual options:
  - On due date
  - Before due date
  - After due date
  - Custom date (lets the user manually choose any date)

This makes it easy to schedule task-related nudges based on deadlines or key project dates.
## **How to Create Reminders**
### **Option 1: Create a Event Reminder from the Navigation Menu**
1. **Click on the Reminder Icon**\
   In the left navigation menu, click the **Reminder** icon (alarm clock symbol).
1. **Open the Reminder Form**\
   A **modal popup** will appear with a form to enter reminder details.
1. **Fill Out the Reminder Details**
   1. **Type**: Choose the type of reminder:
      1. **Event** ‚Äì for personal or general reminders.
      1. **Task/Subtask** ‚Äì if the reminder is tied to a specific work item.
   1. **Title**: Enter the name of the reminder.
   1. **Description** *(Optional)*: Click **Add Description** to include any notes.
   1. **Date**: Select a specific date for the reminder.
   1. **Time**: You can either manually enter the time (hours, minutes, AM/PM) or pick from preset options (11:00 AM, 1:00 PM, 6:00 PM, 9:00 PM).
1. **Click 'Create'**\
   Once the details are entered, click **Create** to save the reminder.
## **Option 2: Create a Reminder from a Task or Subtask**
In 5day.io, you can create reminders that are directly linked to tasks or subtasks. These reminders help ensure you stay aligned with deadlines and follow-ups across your project work.

You can create task or subtask reminders using three methods:

### **üÖê Method A: From the Task List View**
1. Go to the relevant **Project**.
1. Locate the task or subtask in the list.
1. Click the **three-dot menu (‚ãÆ)** next to the item.
1. Select **Set Reminder** from the menu.

### **üÖë Method B: From Inside the Task Panel**
1. Click on the task to open its detailed view.
1. In the task header, click on the **three-dot menu (‚ãÆ)**.
1. Select **Set Reminder** from the dropdown.

### **üÖí Method C: From the Reminder Icon in the Navigation Menu**
1. Click on the **Reminder (alarm clock)** icon from the left navigation bar.
1. In the popup modal, select the **Type** as **Task** or **Subtask**.
1. Select the relevant task or subtask:
   1. **Multiple tasks & subtasks** can be selected at a time.
   1. Selected items will auto-fill the **Title** field.
   1. To remove any selection, click the **‚Äòx‚Äô icon** next to the task or subtask name.

**Note:**\
For all three methods (A, B, and C), once a task or subtask is selected:

- You can optionally click **Add Description** to include notes.
- For **Date**, you‚Äôll get contextual options:
  - On Due Date
  - Before Due Date
  - After Due Date
  - Custom Date (allows selecting any specific date)
- **Time** can be set manually (hours, minutes, AM/PM) or selected from quick options like 11:00 AM, 1:00 PM, 6:00 PM, or 9:00 PM.
- Click **Create** to save the reminder.

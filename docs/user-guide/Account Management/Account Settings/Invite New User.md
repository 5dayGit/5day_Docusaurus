## **Invite a New User**
1. **Go to Account Settings:**\
   Click on your **Profile Icon** and select **Account Settings**.
1. **Open People Section:**\
   Inside Account Settings, click on the **People** tab to manage users.
1. **Click on Invite New:**
   1. In the People section, click on the **Invite New** button.
1. **Choose How to Invite:**\
   You will see two fields to fill:
   1. **Invite by Email:**\
      Enter the email address of the user you want to invite to the 5day.io account.
   1. **Select Role:**\
      Choose the appropriate **Role** for the user (e.g., Account Admin, Manager, Member, External Member, Guest) depending on the level of access they require.
1. **Send the Invitation:**\
   Once you have entered the email address and selected the role, click on **Invite**.
1. **Email Confirmation:**\
   The invited user will receive an **email invitation** to join 5day.io.\
   They will need to accept the invitation and complete their account setup.
1. **User Added:**\
   After accepting the invitation, the user will officially be added to your 5day.io account and assigned the selected role.

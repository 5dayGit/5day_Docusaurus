# Work Schedule

## Overview
In 5day.io, **Work Schedule** is a feature that allows organizations to define the working structure for their members. It helps set up workdays, working hours, and location preferences (such as office or home), creating a clear expectation for when and where team members are available.

With Work Schedule, you can customize:

- Working days
- Start and end times
- Hybrid work options (home or office)
- Calendar week start day
- Holidays
- Multiple work schedule options based on different needs (e.g., regular, hybrid, flexible)

## **Subscription Association**
The **Work Schedule** feature is available across all subscription plans in 5day.io. However, the number of work schedules you can create depends on your subscription plan:

|**Subscription Plan**|**Work Schedule Limit**|
| :-: | :-: |
|Free Plan|Only Default Work Schedule is available|
|Growth Plan|Unlimited Work Schedules can be created|

Who Can Manage Work Schedule 

- **Account Owners** and **Account Admins** with the default system roles can access and manage Work Schedules.
- **Custom Role Users**: Users who have been granted **Manage rights** under **Account Settings** in Global Permissions can also create, edit, and manage Work Schedules.

## **How to Access Work Schedule**
To access the Work Schedule in 5day.io:

1. Click on your **Profile Icon** located at the bottom-left corner of the navigation panel.
1. Select **Account Settings** from the menu.
1. In Account Settings, click on the **General** tab.
1. Under General, you will see the option for **Work Schedule**.
1. Click on **Work Schedule** to view, create, or manage work schedules for your organization.

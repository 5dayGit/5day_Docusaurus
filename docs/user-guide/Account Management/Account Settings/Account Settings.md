## **Overview**
The **Account Settings** section in 5day.io is your organization’s central command hub.\
It’s where you manage and configure everything related to how your workspace functions — from organizational identity to security, user access, and billing.

✅ Think of Account Settings as the **control panel for everything organization-wide** in 5day.io.
##
## **How to Access Account Settings**
To access your organization’s account settings in 5day.io:

1. Click on your **profile icon** in the top or bottom navigation bar (depending on your layout).
1. From the dropdown menu, select **Account Settings**.
1. In the menu, click on the **General** tab.
1. Once you click on **General**, you will see your organization’s account settings page, where you can update your account name, description, logo, and other organizational-level details.

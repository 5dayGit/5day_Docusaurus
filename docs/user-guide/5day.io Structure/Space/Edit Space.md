
# List of Spaces
**List of Spaces** gives you a single, clean screen showing every Space inside the current Workspace - perfect for jumping in, reordering, or managing access in seconds.

## Access List

 - Go to Workspace -> three dots -> **Spaces and Projects**  OR
 - Go to Workspace Views -> List/Board View -> List of Spaces and its associated projects will appear.

## List of Spaces
| Field | Description |
|--|--|
| Icon & Color | Choose a representative icon and color to visually distinguish your space|
| Name | Provide a clear and meaningful name of your  space.|
| Count of Projects| Provides total count of projects associated to space.|

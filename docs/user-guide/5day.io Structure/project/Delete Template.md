### **How to Delete a Template**
To delete a template you no longer need:

1. **Go to Account Settings**: Start by accessing your account settings.
1. **Open Template List**: Navigate to the 'Template List' in the settings.
1. **Select Template**: Next to each template, thereâ€™s a three-dot menu. Click on it and select **Delete**.
1. **Confirm Deletion**: A confirmation prompt will appear. Confirm the deletion to permanently remove the template from the system.

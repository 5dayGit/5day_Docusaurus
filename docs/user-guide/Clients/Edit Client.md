## **How to Edit a Client**
In 5day.io, you can easily edit client information, such as the client name and logo, to keep your records up to date.
### **Steps to Edit a Client:**
1. Go to **Account Settings**.
1. Click on **Clients** from the sidebar.
1. You will see a list of all existing clients.
1. Find the client you want to edit.
1. To edit the **Client Name**:
   1. Click directly on the client’s name.
   1. The name field will become editable.
   1. Update the name as needed.
1. To edit or change the **Client Logo**:
   1. Hover over the current logo.
   1. Click on **Remove Logo**.
   1. Once removed, an **Upload Logo** option will appear.
   1. Upload the new logo.

✅ Changes to the client’s name or logo are automatically saved once updated.

## **How to Change Your Password**
If you’d like to update your login password, follow these steps:

1. Click on your **profile icon** and select **My Settings** from the dropdown.
1. In the **My Settings** menu, go to **Password and Security**.
   ### IMAGE
1. Under the **Change Password** section, click on the **Change Password** button.
1. A form will appear where you need to enter:
   1. Your **current password**
   1. Your **new password**
   1. **Confirm** your new password
1. Click on **Change Password** to save the changes.

✅ Once updated, your new password will take effect immediately.

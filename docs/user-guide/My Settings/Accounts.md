### **Overview**
If you’re part of multiple organizations using 5day.io, the **5day Accounts** section helps you manage your presence across them.\
You can view:

- A list of all the **accounts** you’re part of
- Your **role** in each account
- The option to **leave an account** if needed

This is especially helpful for users who collaborate with multiple organizations.

### **How to Access 5day Accounts**
1. Click on your **profile icon** and select **My Settings**.
1. In the **My Settings** menu, click on **5day Accounts**.
1. You’ll now see a list of all the accounts you're part of, along with your assigned role.
1. To leave an account:
   1. Click on the **three-dot menu** next to the account you want to exit.
   1. Select **Leave Account** and confirm your action.

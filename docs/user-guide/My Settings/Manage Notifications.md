## **How to Enable or Disable Notifications**
In 5day.io, your notification settings give you full control over what updates you want to be alerted for — and how. You can choose to receive notifications through **Email**, **Web**, or **Mobile** for specific actions that matter to you.
###  How to Access Notification Settings
1. Click on your **profile icon** in the top or bottom navigation bar.
1. Select **My Settings** from the dropdown.
1. Click on the **Notifications** tab.

From here, you’ll be able to enable or disable individual notifications based on your role, activity, and preferences.

### **Notification Categories & Events**
Here’s what each category covers:
#### **Projects**
- **Projects I am an Owner**
  - Get notified for **all updates** in a project you own.
  - This includes changes to status, priority, dates, progress, or when someone is added to the project.
#### **Tasks**
- **Tasks Assigned to Me**
  - New task is assigned to you
  - Assignee is changed
  - Task is deleted
  - Task is moved to another section or project
  - Other task updates (e.g., status, priority, type, dates)
- **Tasks Created by Me**
  - When tasks you’ve created are deleted or moved
  - Other task updates in the ones you’ve created
#### **Comments**
- When someone replies to your comment
- New comments in tasks/subtasks **assigned to you**
- New comments in tasks/subtasks **you created**
- When someone **@mentions you**
#### **Timesheet**
- Reminder to **submit** your timesheet
- Reminder for **pending** timesheets
- Reminder to **log** your timesheet
#### **Reminders**
- All general reminders based on your task due dates, timesheets, and project settings

### **How to Configure**
Each row has toggles for:

- **Email**
- **Web**
- **Mobile**

Simply check or uncheck the boxes to enable or disable the notification types you prefer.

✅ Changes are **saved instantly** when you toggle a notification.


**To pause notifications:**

1. In the same **Notifications** section under My Settings,
1. Click the **“Pause notifications”** button.
   1. Once active, it will be highlighted.

**To resume notifications:**

- Simply click the **“Pause notifications”** button again to turn it off.

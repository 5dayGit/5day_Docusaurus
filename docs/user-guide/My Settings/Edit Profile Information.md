## **How to Edit Profile Information**

### **Steps to Edit Your Profile:**
1. Click on your **profile icon** in the navigation bar and go to **My Settings**.
1. Select **Profile** from the My Settings menu.
1. You’ll see two tabs:
   1. **Profile** — for personal details
   1. **Display** — for appearance and layout preferences

From here, you can make the following changes:

- Under the **Profile** tab:
  - First Name, Last Name
  - Phone Number
  - Email Address
  - Date of Birth
  - Profile Picture
- Under the **Display** tab:
  - Time Zone
  - Time Format (12h or 24h)
  - Date Format
  - Duration Format
  - Theme (Light or Dark)
  - Layout (Left or Top navigation)

✅ All updates are saved automatically as soon as you make changes — no manual save is required.

# **My Settings**
## **Overview**
The **My Settings** page in 5day.io allows you to manage your personal preferences and profile settings, helping tailor the platform to your individual work style.

From this page, you can:

- View and update your **profile and time zone** information
- View and switch your **assigned work schedule**
- Manage your **notification preferences**
- **Change your password**
- View all **organizations (accounts)** you're linked to and switch between them if needed

✅ This section ensures you have full control over how you work inside 5day.io, no matter how many teams or organizations you're a part of.

## **How to Access My Settings**
To access your personal settings in 5day.io:

1. Click on your **profile icon** from the top or bottom navigation bar (depending on your layout).
1. From the dropdown menu, select **My Settings**.
1. In the My Settings menu, you’ll see various options such as:
   1. **Profile**
   1. **Notifications**
   1. **Security**
   1. **Work Schedule**
   1. **More**
1. Click on any section you’d like to update directly, or select **More** to view the full list of available settings.

✅ This gives you access to all your personal preferences and allows you to update them at any time.
